Star Tribune Data Academy

This is the syllabus/schedule for the Star Tribune Data Academy sessions -- beginner and advanced.


Beginner Data Academy

Wed. Sept 16

  • Today's goal: Get comfortable navigating Excel and doing basic calculations, filtering and sorting
  • Before class: Read the "Introduction" section of "Data Journalism Handbook"
  • Sign in to Slack, if you haven't already, to join the Data Academy private channel
  • Slack tipsheet
  • Top 10 data journalism rules
  • Reading: What is data journalism?
  • Tipsheet: Basic Calculations in Excel
  • In-class: Our favorite music

Homework

  • Homework: MLB payrolls exercise and data
  • Reading: Finding Data

Wed. Sept 23

  • Today's goal: Discuss finding and requesting data; Learn how to use Excel's importing wizard and Pivot Tables
  • Tipsheet: Using Minnesota's public records law
  • County population exercise and data
  • Tipsheet: Pivot Tables
  • In-class: New Yorker Cartoons exercise and data

Homework

  • Exercise: UMN enrollment data and exercise
  • Reading: Data literacy

Wed. Sept 30

  • Today's goal: Discuss best practices, continue practicing Excel skills, and learn how to make charts in Excel
  • Tipsheet:How to avoid rookie mistakes
  • Tipsheet: Data analysis best practices
  • In-class:Teacher demographics exercise and data

Homework

  • Exercise: Making charts in Excel exercise and data
  • Reading: Read introduction and chapter 1 of "The Functional Art" by Alberto Cairo

Thurs., Oct 8 (note the day change!)

  • Today's goal: Learn how to use Tableau Public for analysis and data visualization
  • Get inspired: Tableau gallery
  • Optional: Tableau training videos

Homework

  • Exercise: Build a real estate data visualization in Tableau exercise and data

Wed. Oct 14

  • Today's goal: Tackle bigger datasets with Excel
  • In-class: St. Paul salaries exercise and data (right-mouse click to download)

Homework

  • Exercise: Met Council salaries exercise and data (right-mouse click to save to your computer) | Record Layout
  • Identify at least two datasets on your beat. What's in it? Who keeps it? How do you get it? Be prepared to discuss in class.

Wed. Oct 21

  • Today's goal: More advanced analysis in Excel and discuss how to incorporate data into beat reporting
  • In-class: Airline passenger exercise and data (right-mouse click to download text file)
  • Tipsheet: Using data in beat reporting
  • Tipsheet: Story ideas by beat
  • Tipsheet: Ready when the story breaks

Homework

  • In-class: Employment exercise and data
  • Reading: Wrestling with data

Wed. Oct 28

  • Today's goal: Learn various ways to wrangle data that isn't ready for analysis
  • In-class: Excel Magic tipsheet and data
  • In-class: School budget data
  • In-class: Insurance exchange data
  • Discussion: Where do I go from here?

Homework
