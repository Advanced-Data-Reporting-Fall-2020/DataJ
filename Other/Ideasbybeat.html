Education:

  • School test scores
  • Languages spoken by students
  • Teacher experience and/or demographics
  • Student demographics
  • Bus drivers with bad records
  • School fire drills
  • Grade inflation -- higher education
  • Teacher and/or administrator salaries


Local/county/state govt:

  • Annual budget
  • Campaign finance of mayor, city council, etc
  • Public officals using the Internet
  • Election results (i.e. How Hatch lost the election)
  • Contracts
  • Govt employee salaries
  • Fraud in use of govt credit cards


Other local govt:

  • Housing inspections
  • Restuarant inspections
  • Kennel inspections
  • Property values/taxes
  • Parking tickets (i.e. related example is the snow emergency snowing in St. Paul)


Crime/Public safety:

  • Fire department recruiting
  • Crime statistics (looking at what types of crimes are occurring, how they have changed from the past and where they are occuring)
  • Racial profiling by police/courts
  • Fire department/ambulance response times


Health Care:

  • Nursing home health & safety violations
  • Hospital ratings
  • Problems with transplant programs


Business/Economy:

  • Workplace safety violations
  • Executive salaries
  • Campaign finance -- business influence on politicians
  • Government contracts
  • Lobbying
  • Foreclosures


Sports:

  • Coach salaries
  • Cost of community college football programs
  • Horse racing injuries
  • How much in student fees goes to college athletic departments
  • Sports participation

Community:

  • Changing demographics
  • Exodus or influx of young people/old people
  • Commuting/transportation -- traffic volumes, money spent on road construction, use of public transit, etc.
